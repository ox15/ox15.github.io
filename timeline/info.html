You can move the timeline (drag mouse over timeline) and zoom in and out (mouse wheel up/down. Clicking on a item will display info in this box, if that item has any info to display. A few things to note:
  • If you can't move your mouse out of the timeline, just click inside it.
  • Some links might not load on older computers because your computer thinks that site's certificate has expired. In Chrome, this shows a gray background with a sad face.
