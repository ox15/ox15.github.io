You can move the timeline (drag mouse over timeline) and zoom in and out (mouse wheel up/down). Clicking on a item will display info in this box, if that item has any info to display. A few things to note:
  • Scrolling on the timeline will zoom in and out. If you want to scroll the page, move the mouse pointer off of the timeline
  • If the timeline keeps moving even after you stop dragging, just click outside of it.
  • Some pages might not load. You'll see "Firefox can't open this page" in Firefox, or a gray background with a in Chrome and Edge. Here's why:
    • If it says "this site can't be reached", your old computer (OSX El Capitan or Windows XP, for example) thinks that site's certificate has expired
    • If it says the site "refused to connect" or something about security, the site doesn't allow itself to be embedded. You can right-click on the link and "open in a new window". Please report these on the form if they are not in the following list.
      • history.com
      • rmg.co.uk
  • You can get back to this info by reloading the page
