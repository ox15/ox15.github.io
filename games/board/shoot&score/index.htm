How to Play Shoot & Score

Materials

  • Shoot & Score board
  • 1-3 game pieces
  • Scoreboard
  • Pencil to record scores

Players

1-3

How long

Set your own time limit.

How to Play

Player 1 flicks (“shoots”) his piece across the board towards the “1000” mark and play continues to Player 2.

Scores for Player 1

Section of board Action
1000 or belowReceive that many points.
Player 1Double your points.
Player 2 or 3Swap points with this player.

Play continues until a set time limit or number of moves. The player with the highest score wins.
