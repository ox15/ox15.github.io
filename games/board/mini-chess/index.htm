Mini Checkers

Time: 15 minutes

Players: 2

Variation 1

Setup

Use 3 pieces for each player and an 8x11 sheet of paper divided into 68 squares. X-mark every other square starting at the top left and going down. The side closest to you is player 1’s side. Number the squares. Viewed from player 1’s side, squares 6½ and 8½ should be 1-way exits for player 2’s pieces. After the first move these squares are treated as X-marked squares. In variation 1, you can’t go on the X-marked squares.

Play

The players alternate turns, moving their pieces diagonally 1 space each turn. The first player to get to #3 (if it’s player 1) or #32 (if it’s player 2) wins. If the other guy is occupying said space, you can push him off the board

Variation 2

Setup

The pieces can go on the X-marked squares. This game is more like mini chess. Use 4 Lego pieces or standard chess pieces.
