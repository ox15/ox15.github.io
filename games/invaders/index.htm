Play a classic invaders game

How to play

  1. Download and install Java
  2. Download the Invaders game
  3. Double-click on the game to play

Controls

Move rocket left & right arrows
Destroy any leftover invaders Enter
Shoot Space
Activate shield Hold Ctrl

Objective

The objective of this game is to shoot all the invaders before they shoot you. Green ones are 1 point, blues are 10, and reds are 100.
